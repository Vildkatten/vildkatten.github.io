Quotify - Write your own quotes

The world's most famous and appreciated quotes compressed into one single app. But that's not all! In this app you can quote yourself and share it on Facebook. Compatible with Android, iPhone and iPod touch. Available for free on App Store and Google Play!

Read

Read the world's most famous and appreciated quotes. Use the randomize button to get a random quote, or search for your favourite author in the extensive database.

Write

The best quotes are often told in your own surroundings, why not write them down using the Quotify app!

Share

Share your own or preinstalled quotes on Facebook. It's easy, it's fun!
