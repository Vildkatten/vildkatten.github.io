Campaign Site

Site made for Idala Vingård and their Swedish julbord. I coded this page from a psd file I got from my designer at Inka Interactive AB. The site is no longer available.

Poetry Slammers

This is an e-magazine where Swedish poetry slammers got their works published in a new way. I collaborated with these fine people, Amanda Reyes, Magnus Waitong, Michael Hansson, Roger Riesbäck and Linus Malmberg. My role in this project were to handle all technical issues, such as sound implementation, transfer text into HTML, CSS fine-tuning, publishing to Appstore. The magazine is unfortunately no longer available in the Appstore.

Quotify

The world's most famous and appreciated quotes compressed into one single app. But that's not all! In this app you can quote yourself and share it on Facebook. Compatible with Android and available for free on Google Play!

Video clip

A collaboration project with Amanda Reyes, David Gaidos, Linus Malmberg and I. My role in this project was directing and filming most of the shots. I was also involved in post production with editing and visual effects. Check it out!
